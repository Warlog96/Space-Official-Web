Regatta 2.0 ✦

Lighter-Than-Air (LTA) vehicle developed for the 'Regatta' competition at IIT Bombay.

Control
RC Propulsion
Event
IIT Bombay
Type
Airship

Regatta 2.0 was a specialized Airship/LTA project. The team designed an aerodynamic envelope and a custom propulsion system to navigate obstacles and complete the race course at the prestigious IIT Bombay Techfest.

Mission Profile
  • Objective: To demonstrate capability and gather data.
  • Status: Mission Completed Successfully.
  • Validation: All systems functioned as designed.
System Architecture
  • Structure: Optimized for stability and weight.
  • Avionics: In-house developed flight computers.
Key Achievements
  • Stable flight throughout boost and coast phases.
  • Successful recovery and payload retrieval.
  • Verified data against theoretical models.
Team & Learning
Venturing into Lighter-Than-Air technology required a completely new skillset. The team mastered envelope aerodynamics, buoyancy control, and precise RC maneuverability to compete at a national level.
