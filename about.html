Our Story

Founded in 2017, Space Club began as a small group of students passionate about aerospace engineering. What started as weekend projects has evolved into a competitive rocketry team participating in national competitions.

What We Do

We design, build, and launch rockets while pushing the boundaries of amateur rocketry. Our projects range from small educational models to advanced high-power rockets capable of reaching altitudes of over 10,000 feet.

Design & Fabrication

Using CAD software and our workshop to bring rocket designs to life.

Research & Development

Experimenting with new materials, propulsion systems, and avionics.

Data Analysis

Collecting and interpreting flight data to improve future designs.

Education & Outreach

Sharing our passion for rocketry with the community through workshops.
